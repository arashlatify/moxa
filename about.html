About us

Learn More About MOXA

Who We Are

MOXA is a youth led educational initiative that helps students build essential digital skills in simple and supportive way. We focus on creating a space where learners can explore technology and build confidence.

Our Mission

Our mission is to make digital literacy accessible for everyone, especially students with limited access to technology.

Why We Exist

Many students struggle with basic digital tools like email and online platforms. MOXA aims to bridge this gap through practical workshops and learning opportunities.
